# What can we learn from Bun's rapid Rust rewrite with AI?

The article discusses Bun's migration from Zig to Rust using the AI tool Fable, which completed the rewrite in 11 days at a cost of $165K, compared to an estimated 1-2 year manual effort. The key enabler was Bun's thoroughly-tested codebase, allowing automated translation with high confidence. The author highlights that such rapid rewrites open new opportunities for developers but require robust test coverage. Fable was temporarily unavailable due to US export controls but is now globally accessible [source].

- Bun's migration from Zig to Rust using Fable cost $165K but reduced timeline from 1-2 years to 11 days.
- The success relied on having a thoroughly-tested project.
- The migration opens new opportunities for developers.
- Fable was temporarily restricted by US export controls but now available globally.
