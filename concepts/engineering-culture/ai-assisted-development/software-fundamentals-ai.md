# Do software fundamentals still matter?

In the age of AI, software fundamentals such as code quality, management of software entropy, and adherence to good design patterns are more critical than ever. This is because AI agents, which are increasingly used to assist in development, perform best in well-structured, clean codebases. The talk emphasizes that building codebases that AI loves to work in requires a strong foundation in traditional software engineering principles, not their abandonment.

- Code quality and good design patterns are more important for AI agents than for human developers alone.
- Software entropy must be actively managed to keep codebases AI-friendly.
- Traditional software fundamentals remain essential in the AI era.
- Building codebases that AI loves to work in improves developer productivity with AI tools.
