# Claude Fable 5 Made Permanent in Max and Team Premium Plans

Anthropic has announced that starting July 20, Claude Fable 5 will be included in all Max and Team Premium plans at 50% of usage limits, reversing a previous plan to remove the model from subscriptions and make it API-only. The decision was driven by competitive pressure from models like GPT-5.6 Sol and Kimi 3, which made it untenable for Anthropic to offer subscriptions without their best model. Pro and Team Standard users retain access via usage credits and receive a one-time $100 credit. The change alleviates concerns about a 'Fablepocalypse' where subscribers would lose access. [Source](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Jul/18/claude-make-fable-5-permanent/#atom-everything)

- Claude Fable 5 will be included in Max ($100/month) and Team Premium ($200/month) plans at 50% of limits starting July 20.
- Pro ($20/month) and Team Standard users get access via usage credits with a one-time $100 credit.
