# LLM cliché highlighter

The author describes building a tool called 'LLM cliché highlighter' (via Fable 5 vibe coding) to identify common patterns in AI-generated writing that have become clichés, such as 'no fluff, no filler, no jargon'. The tool highlights ten such patterns. The project was motivated by the author's frustration with reading numerous articles that rely on these overused phrases. The tool is available online and tagged under tools, AI, generative AI, and LLMs.
